iPhone Configuration Guide

This guide helps you configure your iPhone to manage when and by whom you are disturbed.

1. Managing Notifications

Control which apps send you notifications and how they appear:

  • Go to Settings > Notifications.
  • Tap on the app you want to configure.
  • Customize your notification settings with options like Sounds, Badges, and Banner style.

2. Emergency Bypass for Calls

Allow certain contacts to reach you even when Do Not Disturb is enabled:

  • Open the Contacts app and select a contact.
  • Tap Edit in the top right corner.
  • Scroll to Ringtone or Text Tone, and tap it.
  • Enable Emergency Bypass on the selected tone.

3. Scheduling Quiet Times

Set up Do Not Disturb to automatically engage during specific times:

  • Go to Settings > Do Not Disturb.
  • Enable Scheduled and set the times you prefer.

4. Using Focus Modes

Customize different focus modes for activities like sleep, work, or personal time:

  • Go to Settings > Focus.
  • Select a Focus mode or create a new one by tapping + in the top right corner.
  • Configure your Focus settings, including allowed notifications and people.

5. Sleep Schedule

Set your sleep schedule to automatically turn on Do Not Disturb and limit notifications:

  • Go to Health app > Browse tab > Sleep.
  • Tap Sleep Schedule and set your desired schedule.
